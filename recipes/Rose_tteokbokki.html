Rose Tteokbokki

Rose Tteokbokki is a creamy, spicy Korean dish featuring chewy rice cakes in a rich, velvety gochujang and cream sauce. Perfect for a comforting, flavorful meal!

Here's the HTML code for Rose Tteokbokki, following a similar structure:

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of Korean rice cake (tteok)
  • 1 brown onion (optional)
  • 1 spring onion (optional)
  • 150 grams of fish cakes
  • any kind of noodle you like (optional)

    • Sauce

  • 2 spoons of gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 spoon of sugar
  • 2 spoons of soy sauce
  • 3 spoons of heavy cream
  • 1 spoon of gochugaru (Korean chili powder)
  • half spoon of curry powder (optional)
  • 400 ml of water

Steps

  1. Cut the onion and spring onion, then stir-fry them until the onion becomes transparent.
  2. Pour 400 ml of water and add all the ingredients for the sauce (order doesn't matter).
  3. Add the rice cakes and fish cakes.
  4. Put the noodles (if you have any) 1 minute after the previous step.
  5. Let it cook for 2 more minutes, then mix in the heavy cream.
  6. Enjoy your Rose Tteokbokki!
